Typing in the wrong language wastes time and breaks focus — this app fixes that. It runs silently in the background, with optional auto-start on Windows. No setup or dependencies — just launch and type seamlessly. Perfect for bilingual users who want speed, simplicity, and control.

Open the app and use the keyboard shortcut Ctrl+M to toggle languages.
That’s it — the switch is instant, and the app stays out of your way.
